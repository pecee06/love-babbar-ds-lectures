1. Prime numbers
    Natural numbers greater than 1 which are only divisible by 1 and itself are primes
    Eg. 2,3,5,7,11,...

2. Greatest Common Divisor
    GCD of 2 natural numbers is the maximum number which divides both the numbers

3. Modulo
    % calculates the remainder
    m%n lies in the range [0,n-1]
    % obeys distributive law
